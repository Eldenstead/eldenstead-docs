# SUP-004 — Account and Login Problems

> New to staff work? See the [Plain-language glossary](../00_PLAIN_LANGUAGE_GLOSSARY.md) for technical terms.

## 1. Purpose

Help with account and login problems without weakening account security.

## 2. Scope

Login failures, whitelist/ban confusion, account linking, changed Minecraft names, website access, Discord membership, resource-pack access, and suspected account compromise.

## 3. Who does what

- **Support:** Troubleshoots normal access and gathers identifiers.
- **Moderator:** Confirms punishment status and ban-evasion concerns.
- **Administrator:** Reviews server-side permissions and account mappings.
- **Security/Founder:** Handles suspected compromise, impersonation, or high-risk identity disputes.

## 4. Procedure

### 4.1 Identify the affected service
Confirm whether the problem is Minecraft authentication, server connection, Discord, website, store, map, forum, or another service. Record exact error wording and time.

### 4.2 Basic checks
- Confirm the official address/link.
- Check known outages and maintenance.
- Confirm supported Minecraft version and required resource-pack settings.
- Verify the player's current Minecraft name and UUID where available.
- Check whitelist, ban, mute, permission, or account-link status through authorized tools.
- Ask the player to retry safe client-side steps such as restarting, relogging, or clearing a cached server resource pack only when relevant.

### 4.3 Identity boundaries
Eldenstead cannot reset Microsoft/Minecraft, Discord, email, bank, or payment-provider passwords. Direct players to the official provider recovery process. Never accept another person's account login details as proof.

### 4.4 Name changes and linking
Use UUID-based records when possible. Before moving store purchases, forum identity, or linked records, verify continuity through server history/provider records and ensure the old identity is not a separate person.

### 4.5 If an account may be hacked
1. Advise the player to secure the underlying platform account first.
2. Temporarily restrict high-risk Eldenstead actions when authorized.
3. Preserve login, command, trade, claim, and punishment records.
4. Do not reveal IP addresses or login fingerprints to the claimant.
5. Restore access or purchases only after reasonable ownership verification and review of damage.

### 4.6 Ban versus access issue
Do not troubleshoot around an active ban. Provide the recorded reason and appeal route. Suspected ban evasion is handled under MOD-004.

## 5. When to get senior help

Escalate competing ownership claims, compromised staff accounts, changed UUID mappings, suspicious transfer of store purchases, ban-evasion networks, or any request to expose login/IP data.

## 6. What to record

- Affected service and error
- Player identifiers/UUID
- Outage and status checks
- Permission/linking findings
- Identity verification steps
- Access correction or escalation

## 7. Final checklist

- [ ] Correct service identified
- [ ] Exact error and time recorded
- [ ] Known outage checked
- [ ] UUID/account mapping reviewed
- [ ] No external password recovery attempted
- [ ] Ban status handled through moderation
- [ ] Compromise evidence preserved where needed
- [ ] Final access state documented
