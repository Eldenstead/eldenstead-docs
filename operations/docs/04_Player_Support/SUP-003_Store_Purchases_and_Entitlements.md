# SUP-003 — Store Purchases and Missing Items or Perks

> New to staff work? See the [Plain-language glossary](../00_PLAIN_LANGUAGE_GLOSSARY.md) for technical terms.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Support staff must never promise a refund or ask a player to send full card or banking information.

## 1. Purpose

Fix missing purchases and store delivery problems while keeping payment information safe. Store items must also follow Eldenstead's non-pay-to-win promise.

## 2. Scope

Purchases made through `eldenstead.com/store`, cosmetic items or perks, ranks, missing delivery, duplicate delivery, chargebacks, refund requests, and store configuration issues.

## 3. Who does what

- **Support:** Collects non-sensitive transaction details and checks delivery status.
- **Billing owner:** Reviews provider records, fulfills valid purchases, and handles disputes.
- **Developer/Administrator:** Repairs technical delivery issues without altering payment records.
- **Founder:** Approves refunds, policy exceptions, product changes, and chargeback responses.

## 4. Procedure

### 4.1 Check the player and purchase
Request only:
- Minecraft account name/UUID used at checkout
- Approximate purchase date/time
- Product name
- Order or transaction reference from the provider
- Screenshot of confirmation with unrelated payment details hidden

Never ask for a full card number, bank login, CVV, password, login code, or full billing statement in Discord.

### 4.2 Check delivery
1. Confirm the payment status in the authorized provider dashboard.
2. Confirm the account identifier entered at purchase.
3. Review delivery logs/commands and current purchase status.
4. Check whether the player changed their Minecraft name or linked the wrong account.
5. Verify that the product still exists and is compatible with current permissions/resource pack.

### 4.3 Deliver or fix the purchase
- **Paid and not delivered:** Re-run the approved delivery process or grant the exact purchased product manually with a record.
- **Wrong account:** Correct only after ownership is reasonably verified and the product has not been transferred/consumed in a way that creates duplication.
- **Duplicate delivery:** Remove only the duplicate, preserving the valid purchase.
- **Pending/failed payment:** Do not grant based solely on a screenshot; direct the player to the payment provider for the current status.

### 4.4 Refund requests
Eldenstead's posted terms say purchases are normally non-refundable. Support staff must not promise or reject a refund exception on their own. Record the request and send it to the Billing Owner or Founder. They must consider payment-provider rules, local law, duplicate charges, fraud, and purchases that were not delivered.

### 4.5 Payment disputes and fraud
Do not confront or threaten the player. Keep the order, messages, delivery logs, and login records. Staff may temporarily remove a disputed purchase when approved. Do not punish the player through an unrelated moderation action. A Founder handles the payment-provider reply.

### 4.6 Keep store products fair
Store items must stay cosmetic or harmless to gameplay. A Founder must review any product that could affect combat, the economy, claims, war, progress, or access to rare resources before it is sold.

## 5. When to get senior help

Escalate refunds, chargebacks, fraud, minors' purchase concerns, provider account problems, leaked payment data, repeated delivery failures, or any product that may be pay-to-win.

## 6. What to record

- Transaction reference and status
- Account/UUID
- Product and expected purchase
- Delivery logs and corrections
- Refund/chargeback decision
- Approver and player communication

## 7. Final checklist

- [ ] Identity and transaction reference checked
- [ ] No prohibited payment data requested
- [ ] Provider status verified
- [ ] Delivery logs reviewed
- [ ] Exact purchase delivered or corrected
- [ ] Refund/chargeback escalated appropriately
- [ ] Non-pay-to-win review applied
- [ ] Ticket record completed
