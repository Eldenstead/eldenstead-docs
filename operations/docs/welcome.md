# Staff SOP Handbook

> **Version 1.1 — July 22, 2026**  
> **Audience:** Staff and helpers ages 13 and up.

---

## What this handbook is for

This handbook gives Eldenstead staff one clear way to handle daily work, player problems, emergencies, updates, new content, and team decisions.

It covers:

- The Minecraft server and EldenKingdoms
- Paper plugins and Citizens NPCs
- The custom resource pack and Squaremap
- Discord, the website, forums, and social media
- Staff hiring, training, moderation, support, and security

A new staff member should be able to follow these guides without needing someone beside them. Commands, plugin names, and panels may change over time. When that happens, update the technical details through the SOP change guide.

## Who should read it

This handbook is for Helpers, Moderators, Builders, Developers, Administrators, Managers, Owners, event staff, lore staff, and approved outside workers.

Some tasks are simple. Others require training or senior approval. Never use a command, panel, account, or permission that your role does not allow.

## Main rules

1. **Protect the community first.** Player safety, fairness, privacy, and server stability come before convenience.
2. **Use the safest action.** Choose the action that fixes the problem while causing the least disruption.
3. **Write down important decisions.** Records help staff review what happened and improve later.
4. **Keep unreleased content private.** Staff access does not give permission to share future features, builds, lore, dates, or team discussions.
5. **Do not promise what you cannot approve.** This includes refunds, item replacement, unbans, release dates, promotions, or new features.
6. **Keep facts separate from opinions.** Save logs, screenshots, times, and test results before deciding what happened.
7. **Get senior help early when risk is high.** Security problems, data loss, payment disputes, staff misconduct, and major exploits need senior review.
8. **Keep Eldenstead non-pay-to-win.** Store items must not give an unfair gameplay advantage.
9. **Treat people with respect.** Be calm, clear, fair, and professional even when another person is upset.
10. **Ask before guessing.** When a rule or approval limit is unclear, pause and contact the right lead.

## Suggested staff structure

| Role | Main job | What they can usually approve |
|---|---|---|
| Founders / Owners | Final direction, money, legal choices, and live-server access | Final approval |
| Staff Manager | Hiring, training, staff conduct, and staff problems | Staff actions and team coordination |
| Administrator | Senior moderation and live-server work | Longer punishments, incident leadership, and approved recovery |
| Developer | Code, plugins, settings, testing, and releases | Approved test and live-server changes |
| Builder / Lore Team | Builds, atmosphere, official lore, and NPC content | Approved work in their area |
| Moderator | Rule enforcement and player safety | Warnings, mutes, kicks, and some temporary bans |
| Helper / Support | Questions, tickets, and first review | Information, ticket routing, and evidence collection |
| Event / Content Team | Events, screenshots, videos, and announcements | Approved event and media work |

> [!NOTE]
> Give each role only the access it needs. Trust alone is not a reason to give someone console, billing, database, or owner access.

## How to use these guides

1. Open the guide that best matches the task.
2. Check that your role allows you to do it.
3. Read the whole procedure before using commands or changing data.
4. Follow the steps in order unless doing so would cause more harm during an emergency.
5. Save the required evidence, approvals, and final result.
6. Ask senior staff for help when the guide says to do so.
7. Finish the checklist at the end.

## Problem levels

| Level | What it means | Examples | How fast to respond |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SEV-1 Critical** | Accounts, data, or the server are in immediate danger | Hacked owner account, destructive exploit, major data loss, server-wide outage | Act and contact senior staff at once |
| **SEV-2 High** | Many players are affected or the issue is serious | Economy dupe, repeated crashes, failed store delivery for many players | Begin as soon as trained senior staff are available |
| **SEV-3 Medium** | The issue has limited impact or a safe workaround | Broken command, grief in one area, forum problem | Normal staff response |
| **SEV-4 Low** | The issue is visual, informational, or not urgent | Typo, small NPC problem, suggestion | Add it to the task list |

## Who maintains the handbook

The **Staff Manager** keeps the handbook organized. The **Founders** approve changes involving staff power, punishments, payments, privacy, security, or access to the live server. Technical guides should also be checked by the lead Developer or Server Administrator.

Review each SOP at least every six months. Review it sooner after a serious incident, large update, rule change, or change in staff roles.
