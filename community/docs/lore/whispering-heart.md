# The Whispering Heart

The Whispering Heart is a lore ritual built around a bound lectern and temporary conversation circle.

## Ritual Behavior

- The active circle includes players already within range when opened.
- Participant chat stays inside the ritual session.
- Scripted lore responses may answer known secrets.
- Staff may send participant-only omens during an active session.
- Sessions close when participants leave or the configured timeout is reached.
