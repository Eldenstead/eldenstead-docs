# Kingdoms Overview

Kingdoms are player-run governments with land, ranks, treasuries, plots, relations, resource nodes, and war participation.

## Kingdom Systems

- Creation, invites, ownership, titles, and mottos
- Chunk claims and capital claims
- Rank permissions and trusted plots
- Kingdom and empire chat channels
- Diplomacy, treaties, enemies, and alliances
- Resource nodes and regional bonuses
- Conquest wars managed by staff

Kingdom leadership should document public laws, taxes, building standards, and diplomacy expectations on the forum.
