# Messenger Birds

Messenger birds replace instant private messages with distance-aware delivery.

## Player Flow

- Send a private message with `/msg <player> <message>`.
- Reply with `/reply <message>` or `/r <message>`.
- Delivery time depends on distance and world.
- Offline deliveries may be held until the target player rejoins.

Use messenger birds for roleplay-friendly private communication rather than instant out-of-character coordination.
